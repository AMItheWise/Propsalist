# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects respectful, constructive collaboration.

Participants should:

- communicate clearly and professionally
- focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior, not people
- welcome good-faith feedback
- assume positive intent until evidence suggests otherwise
- help keep discussions accessible to contributors with different experience levels

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable:

- harassment, threats, or discrimination
- personal attacks, insults, or hostile sarcasm
- deliberate intimidation or exclusion
- publishing private information without permission
- spam, trolling, or repeated bad-faith disruption

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, pull
requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from
the project spaces.

## Reporting

To report conduct issues, open a private security-style report if appropriate or
contact the repository maintainer directly through GitHub.
